Frontend Frameworks and Libraries
Angular
Angular is a popular, open-source, and comprehensive front-end framework for building complex web applications. It is maintained by Google and offers a wide range of features, including declarative templates, dependency injection, end-to-end testing, and more. Angular uses TypeScript, a superset of JavaScript, to provide strong typing and other advanced features for large-scale projects.
